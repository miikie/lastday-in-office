### **Last Day in the Office**

Everyone remembers their first day.
No one really talks about the last.

DefinitelyNotEvil Mart runs a fairly ordinary internal platform: orders, carts, payments, dashboards, the usual corporate machinery. Hundreds of commits, dozens of contributors, nothing that stands out at first glance. Recently, though, things have felt… off.

A junior employee left the company under tense circumstances. Access was revoked, tickets reassigned, and management insists everything is business as usual. Still, strange artifacts keep appearing. Changes that dont quite make sense. Records that vanish. Processes that complete, but leave nothing behind.

You have been asked to take a closer look.

Somewhere in this project is something that was never meant to stick around. Whatever it is, it doesnt want to be seen and it wont wait patiently for you to find it.

Your task is simple:
**recover what was left behind before its gone for good.**..
